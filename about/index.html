Our Salesforce Journey

Our experience with Salesforce.com began in 2008, while implementing Salesforce at AtTask, now WorkFront. A few years later, as the Senior Director of Information Systems, we were certified as a Salesforce Administrator and led a CRM conversion from SugarCRM to Salesforce in an organization of over 500 employees.

Now, with over 13 years of experience on the platform, we have built a team of experts, with many years of experience and certified in multiple aspects of the Salesforce platform, from Administration to Development and from Implementations to Integrations.

Our Mission: Own Every Aspect of Salesforce

We want to own Salesforce Consulting; Salesforce Administration; Salesforce Migration; Salesforce Integration; Salesforce Development; Salesforce Training; Salesforce AI. This comprehensive expertise allows us to deliver complete solutions that transform your Salesforce investment into measurable success.

Our approach is different. We believe in building capabilities, not dependencies. Our proven "train your team" methodology ensures that your organization becomes self-sufficient while achieving 100% user adoption.

Proven Results

Our work with the LA Chamber of Commerce demonstrates our methodology in action: transforming 62.5% user adoption to 100%, training 36 employees, and completing complex integrations that others couldn't deliver. We've successfully served 50+ clients across California and nationwide.

Today, we serve nonprofits and businesses across California and nationwide, delivering measurable results through our comprehensive Salesforce expertise and commitment to client success.
