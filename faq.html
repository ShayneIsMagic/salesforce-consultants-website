Implementation & Setup

How much does Salesforce implementation cost?

Salesforce implementation costs vary significantly based on several factors:

  • Basic Implementation: $15,000 - $30,000 (up to 10 users)
  • Standard Implementation: $30,000 - $60,000 (10-50 users)
  • Enterprise Implementation: $60,000 - $100,000+ (50+ users)

Factors affecting cost include:

  • Number of users and licenses
  • Customization requirements
  • Integration needs
  • Data migration complexity
  • Training requirements

We offer free assessments to provide accurate pricing based on your specific needs.

How long does Salesforce implementation take?

Implementation timelines depend on complexity and scope:

  • Basic Setup: 4-6 weeks
  • Standard Implementation: 6-10 weeks
  • Enterprise Implementation: 10-16 weeks

Our proven 5-step process ensures efficient delivery:

  1. Assessment & Strategy (1-2 weeks)
  2. Design & Planning (1-2 weeks)
  3. Implementation (2-8 weeks)
  4. Training & Adoption (1-2 weeks)
  5. Optimization (ongoing)
What is included in Salesforce implementation?

Our comprehensive implementation includes:

  • System Setup: User configuration, security settings, and data structure
  • Customization: Fields, objects, workflows, and automation
  • Data Migration: Import and clean existing data
  • Integration: Connect with existing systems and tools
  • Training: Administrator and end-user training
  • Documentation: Process guides and user manuals
  • Go-Live Support: Launch assistance and troubleshooting

Technical & Integration

What Salesforce products do you work with?

We work with the full Salesforce ecosystem:

  • Sales Cloud: Lead and opportunity management
  • Service Cloud: Customer service and support
  • Marketing Cloud: Email marketing and automation
  • Community Cloud: Partner and customer portals
  • Platform: Custom application development
  • Analytics: Reporting and business intelligence

We also integrate with popular third-party tools like QuickBooks, Mailchimp, and other business applications.

How do you handle data migration?

Our systematic data migration process ensures zero data loss:

  1. Data Assessment: Review existing data structure and quality
  2. Mapping Strategy: Plan field mappings and transformations
  3. Data Cleaning: Remove duplicates and standardize formats
  4. Test Migration: Validate data in sandbox environment
  5. Production Migration: Execute final data transfer
  6. Verification: Confirm data integrity and completeness

We support migration from various sources including Excel, Access, other CRMs, and custom databases.
